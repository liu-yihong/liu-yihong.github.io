Running 🏃

I used to be a fat boy since I started my first year in SHUFE. Some day in a winter, I found myself quite out of breath while stepping upstairs. How uncomfortable and unhealthy would I be after 10 years if I keep these fat along with my body? I just told my self I gotta keep slim and I started to execrise.

Fisrt, I decided to run around the campus and during long vacations I ran in a park near my home.



Every time I run, 5 km was a baseline. And in 2017, it took me nearly 63.5 hours, which is 570 km in total. I tried half marathon (21 km) twice and I really hope I can challenge a ful marathon myself someday.
